Hinata Aoi

  1. Hinata Aoi is the leader of idol group 2wink, part of the agency CosPro.
  2. He is one of two members in his unit, the other being his younger brother, Yuta Aoi.
  3. He is a second year at Yumenosaki High School, in class 1a.
  4. He is friends with Shinobu Sengoku and Tetora Nagumo.
  5. Hinata is known to be enthusiastic, often playing pranks on others, as well as his seniors.

Midori Takamine

  1. Midori Takamine is a third year student of Yumenosaki High School.
  2. He is the fourth member of the Super Sentai based unit 'Ryuseitai', known as 'Ryuseitai Green'.
  3. He is often very pessimistic, as he enrolled in the idol course on accident, and thus, does not want to be there.
  4. Occasionally, he does modeling.
  5. He has come to accept and embrace his fate as an idol, and works just as hard as the rest of Ryuseitai's members.

Mika Kagehira

  1. Mika Kagehira is the second member of two-person unit Valkyrie.
  2. He is a third year student of Yumenosaki High School, and is known to be rather brainless at times.
  3. Despite how he often lacks thought, he is very caring towards others, and is very devoted towards Shu Itsuki.
  4. He sometimes repairs stuffed animals for his juniors.
  5. He once tried to bury Ritsu Sakuma, believeing he'd killed him (Ritsu was asleep).
